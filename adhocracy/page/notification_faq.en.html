What can I watch?

You can watch activities regarding proposals, tags, instances and users. Watching means that you are notified of any changes in the respective area.

How can I watch something?

To get notifications click on the watch button in the upper right corner.

How can I set how many notifications I want to get?

Click on the Settings link in the upper right corner to get to your profile page. Under Notifications you can choose how detailed you want your notifications to be.

Via what channel can I be notified?

Notifications via e-mail and via twitter are implemented so far. More possibilities will be added in the future.

What is my Watchlist?

In your watchlist (link in the upper right corner) you can see which proposals, users, instances and tags you are watching.
